About Pantheon

Built by Engineers Who Saw a Better Way

"We ascend beyond traditional methods - using precision technology to protect buildings, reduce environmental impact, and deliver results that make property owners proud."

Pantheon was founded in Calgary in 2022 by Ali Hamidou - after he watched a window cleaner dangling 18 stories above Macleod Trail in a windstorm and thought: there is absolutely a better way to do this. Ali had spent years at the intersection of technology and real estate, and couldn't reconcile why an industry managing billions in commercial assets was still sending people up ropes to clean glass.

He built Pantheon to fix that. Based in Burnaby and serving commercial properties across BC, Alberta, and beyond, Pantheon uses purpose-built industrial cleaning drones to deliver safer, faster, and more cost-effective exterior maintenance.

Transport Canada RPAS Certified - Our pilots hold Transport Canada drone certification. Every commercial operation is filed and executed to full regulatory standard.

Genuine environmental commitment - Biodegradable, water-minimizing solutions certified safe for stormwater. Every project includes documented water savings data for ESG reporting.

Full digital documentation - 4K before/after footage and a comprehensive QC report on every project. Assets your team, insurer, and board can actually use.
